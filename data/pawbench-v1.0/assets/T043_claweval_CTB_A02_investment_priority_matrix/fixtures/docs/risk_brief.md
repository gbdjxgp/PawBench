# Risk Brief

## Project A

- Focus area: Regional channel upsell
- Has 2 stable pilot customers, but further expansion depends on the pace of new distributor training
- Commercial terms are relatively mature with no new regulatory hurdles
- Main uncertainty lies in channel execution speed, not technology or compliance

Risk conclusion: `Medium risk`

## Project B

- Focus area: Expansion into a newly regulated market
- Expected highest returns, but requires a new supplier to deliver critical equipment
- Target market has stricter entry approvals with uncertain timing
- If a price war occurs, profit margins will decrease noticeably

Risk conclusion: `High risk`

## Project C

- Focus area: Enterprise edition upgrade for an existing product line
- Targeting the existing customer base with renewal and expansion intent already under contract
- No new sensitive data processing workflows; compliance requirements follow the existing framework
- Delivery dependencies are mainly internal product integration with manageable complexity

Risk conclusion: `Low risk`
